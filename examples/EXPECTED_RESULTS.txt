EasyHap 1.0 demonstration dataset

Shared inputs:
- sample_group.tsv: 120 samples (60 Cultivar and 60 Landrace)
- traits.tsv: two numeric traits with designed haplotype effects
- demo.gff3: one complete gene model at ChrDemo:1000-2800
- regions.tsv: ChrDemo:900-2900

VCF inputs:
- demo_haploid_multiallelic.vcf: 120 haploid samples
- demo_diploid_multiallelic.vcf: 120 phased diploid samples
- demo_triploid_multiallelic.vcf: 120 phased triploid samples

Expected analysis settings:
- Fisher comparison: Cultivar versus Landrace
- Fisher alpha: 0.05
- Fisher adjustment: none
- Cluster threshold: 0.20
- Region: ChrDemo:900-2900

Expected results for each VCF after Fisher filtering:
- Input variants: 8
- Retained variants: 6
- Raw haplotypes: 6
- Haplotype clusters: 3
- At least one raw haplotype is represented by fewer than 5 samples
- Plant_height and Seed_weight show strong differences among major haplotype/cluster classes
